Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 7, 2024

Welcome to our Privacy Policy page! When you use our app, you trust us with your information. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. This is important; we hope you will take time to read it carefully.

Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Email Address: We collect and store the email address of participants to provide services related to NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme).
  • Camera Access: We access your camera to allow you to add a profile image in your app account.
  • Location Data: We use your location data to help you locate providers near you.

How We Use Information We Collect

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide NDIS services to participants.
  • Enable participants to add and update their profile images.
  • Help participants find providers in their vicinity.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except to comply with legal obligations or to protect and defend our rights and property.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free. Please keep this in mind when disclosing any information to us via the internet.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us at support@example.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
